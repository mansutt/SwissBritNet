    2026 Exhibition at Basel University Library 2026

    In spring 2026, Basel University Library will host an exhibition curated by the Basel SwissBritNet team. Records, books, letters and material artefacts from the rich collections of Basel University Library will document the experience of travellers, scholars, chaplains and other Swiss anglophiles in the 17th and 18th century. The Basel scholar Johann Jacob Frey will feature prominently since his correspondence with English churchmen, aristocrats, antiquaries and scholars presents a singular testimony to the close confessional, political and scholarly entanglement of early modern Anglo-Swiss exchange.

    2023 SwissBritNet Kick-Off-Conference

    On October 6-7, 2023, the SwissBritNet team, project partners and critical friends met in Basel to launch the project officially. Researchers, teachers and students discussed texts, databases and technical issues.

    2022 SwissBritNet at SKILLNET

    In November 2022, Ina Habermann and Lukas Rosenthaler introduced SwissBritNet at the SKILLNET Conference in Utrecht in a paper on "Research-driven data modeling and the development of intelligent queries". Project partner Sepideh Alassi presented the Bernoulli-Euler Online Project (BEOL).
